### 1. Life Origin Journey 🧬

Status:
Future exploration layer

Concept:

Show the possible journey from chemistry to life:

Chemistry
↓
RNA
↓
DNA
↓
Cell
↓
Life
↓
Human


Notes:

- DNA is familiar to most people.
- Add RNA because RNA may have played an important role in early life.
- Use careful wording:
  "One hypothesis is that RNA existed before modern DNA-based life."


Purpose:

Help users understand not only the size of life,
but the story of how life developed.


Priority:

After CORE Earth experience is complete.
